Schedule & Status

Generate a weekly schedule to control when the character is online/offline. Used for status display and autonomous messaging.

Custom Game Time

Set a custom in-game time that can advance per message, in real-time, or manually. Displayed in the phone's status bar.

Autonomous Messaging

When enabled (via schedule), the character can send messages on their own after a period of inactivity from the user.
